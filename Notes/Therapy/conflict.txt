## Working through a conflict or issue ##
-----------------------------------------
1. Identify and name specific problem or conflict
2. Reflect on it
3. Name the core issue.
4. Discuss it, ask for feedback.
5. Use that to work on specific conflict and feelings at a deeper level.
6. Talk and/ or write some more about it.
7. Meditate upon it or pray about it.
8. Consider how you might learn from it.
9. If you still feel incomplete, repeat any of the above.
10. Whenever, you are ready. Let it go.
